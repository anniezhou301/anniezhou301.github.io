Cinnamon Is My Favorite Spice

In the vegetable soup of life, variety would be the oregano, the thyme, the paprika, the basil. The carrots are going to be bland if you don’t add salt; the chickpeas tasteless without black pepper. Not falling into routine, and actively seeking out new experiences is what makes life exciting and interesting, and more or less worth living. I have enough trouble eating vegetable soup as it is, I have no desire to try it cut down to a dull watery paste that Gordon Ramsey wouldn’t even use to feed his chickens. I try my hardest to keep new experiences bountiful in my life, and have found it to be more or less a key to happiness.
